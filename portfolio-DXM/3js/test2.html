HOGA - Productivity App

HOGA is a web-based productivity dashboard with a complex customization system yet intuitive and easy to use UI, designed to promote time management efficiency and good working/studying habits. The dashboard allows users to have all the necessary implements and 'mini-apps' one usually accesses for focus sessions.

Our app HOGA is currently a contender for a government-funded kickstarter seeding program in Seoul (A Big Think 2024), having captured the interest of various local tech investors.

L/F:: JavaScript/HTML/CSS, Python, Flask, SQL, JQuery, Three.js

FitLogger - Fitness App

FitLogger is a React.js project currently being made in collaboration with Luke Schmit, primary for personal usage.

The goal of the App is to provide its users with a highly modular interface allowing them to freely plan and follow weekly diet, exercise and other athletic-related goals. Thanks to React's component and hooks system, FitLogger will be able to provide a very comprehensive tracker module that will let users switch from imperial to metric, change daily work-out sessions on the fly, and share their plans with anyone, be it friends, students or coach.

L/F:: JavaScript/HTML/CSS, React, Node.js

Psychophobia - Game

Based on the world of the movie STALKER by Andrei Tarkovsky and the subsequent, Psychophobia is a fan-project entirely made on JavaScript. I've started this game from scratch in April 2024, back when I just started learning coding. The purpose being to practice my first language (JS/HTML/CSS) and get familiar with new concepts by integrating them into this project as I learn them.

Currently all the foundations and core system of a classic RPG game have been implemented: Inventory, level and equipment system, Randomized loot, Asynchronous combat, Multiple factions with reputation as well as a roguelite approach for replayability and gameplay diversity.

L/F: JavaScript/HTML/CSS
